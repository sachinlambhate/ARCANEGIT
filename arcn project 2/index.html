About me

"Hailing from the vibrant city of Nagpur, I am a seasoned singer-songwriter with over a thousand live performances under my belt. My journey in music has been a thrilling adventure, and I've had the privilege of sharing my passion for music with audiences far and wide. Nagpur's rich cultural heritage has undoubtedly influenced my musical style, infusing it with a unique blend of tradition and western music. With each performance, I strive to connect with my audience through heartfelt lyrics and melodious tunes, leaving a lasting impression on everyone who listens. My music journey is a testament to the power of dedication and a deep love for the art of songwriting and performing."
