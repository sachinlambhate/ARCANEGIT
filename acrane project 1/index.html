Our Taste

Earthy

Coffee with earthy notes often has flavors reminiscent of soil or forest floor. It can be robust and slightly woody, making it a unique and natural-tasting coffee.

Nutty

Nutty coffees have flavors akin to nuts, such as almonds, hazelnuts, or cashews. These coffees are often smooth and mild, with a pleasant, roasted nuttiness.

chocolatey

Fruity

Fruity coffees offer a spectrum of fruit flavors, from bright citrus to sweet berry notes.

Floral

Floral coffees have aromatic and floral notes, reminiscent of flowers like jasmine, lavender, or honeysuckle.

Herbaceous

Herbaceous coffees offer flavors akin to fresh herbs, such as basil or mint. They can provide a refreshing and unique taste experience.

Citrusy

Citrusy coffees feature the zesty and bright flavors of citrus fruits like lemon, orange, or grapefruit. They are often found in lighter roast coffees.

Spicy

Spicy coffee can have a range of spice-related flavors, including cinnamon, cardamom, or even peppery notes..
